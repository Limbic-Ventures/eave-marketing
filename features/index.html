Features

Everything Eave does — your home's memory, and memory that acts.

Eave is one place for your home's records, projects, and what needs doing next. It remembers the details you can never find later, then uses them to tell you what matters now. Here are the six parts that make that work.

Features · Capture

Notice it once. Eave turns it into a plan.

Speak it, snap it, or type a half-sentence. In seconds Eave scopes the whole job — the room, the category, the priority, an estimated cost, and whether you'll need a permit — and files it where you'll find it. A note becomes something you can act on this afternoon.

Learn more about Capture →

Features · Home Knowledge Base

Everything about your home, in one place you'll find later.

Every room, appliance, contractor, and repair — a complete, searchable record of your home. Specs get researched for you down to the furnace filter size, warranties are tracked, and two years from now the answer is already there.

Learn more about the Home Knowledge Base →

Features · Connected Sources

Your inbox already has your home's history.

Connect Gmail and your calendar today, and Eave surfaces the receipts, warranties, and contractor confirmations already sitting in your inbox — read-only, and nothing saves until you review it. Store and smart-home accounts are rolling out next.

Learn more about Connected Sources →

Features · Upkeep Score

A single number for how your home is actually doing.

A live 0–100 score that reflects your home's real health — open projects, overdue tasks, and the age of your systems — not a generic checklist. Watch it move before you lift a finger, so you always know whether something can wait.

Learn more about the Upkeep Score →

Features · Intelligence

Eave tells you what to do next, and why it matters now.

Seasonal prep before the season turns, and a weekly action queue of up to five things that actually need you — each ranked, reasoned, and scoped to your property. The optional upgrade that turns your records into a plan.

Learn more about Intelligence →

Features · Guardian

More active than insurance — protection for the home you already have.

An evidence vault with one-tap insurance and warranty claim packets, a 10-year capex forecast, a carrier-discount checklist, an annual virtual audit review, and telemetry fusion for leak, electrical, solar, and thermostat sensors — the protection layer on top of your records.

Learn more about Guardian →
