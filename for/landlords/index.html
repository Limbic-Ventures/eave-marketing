For landlords & owners

Every property's upkeep, under one account.

Rentals don't forget the deferred repair — the tenant does, until it becomes urgent. Eave keeps a complete record for each home you own: what it has, what it needs, who fixed it last, and what it cost.

The owner's problem

More properties, more places for upkeep to slip

Owning rentals means holding more homes in your head than any one person reasonably can. Which unit's water heater is on its last year. Who you called for the roof at the duplex. What the furnace filter size is at the place across town. When a repair gets deferred, it quietly compounds — and a deferred repair costs an average of $5,600 once it finally becomes urgent.

Eave gives every property its own record, so upkeep doesn't depend on memory or a shoebox of receipts. Below is what you can do per property, and how Eave Multi-Property manages several at once.

Per property, today

A full record for every home you own

Records that don't rely on memory

Each property gets a searchable home knowledge base — rooms, appliances, and their specs. Model numbers, purchase dates, warranty expirations, and service intervals, researched down to the furnace filter size so you're not guessing at the hardware store.

Projects and maintenance, tracked

Capture a repair the moment a tenant reports it, and Eave scopes it into a project with a room, category, priority, and estimated cost. Seasonal maintenance tasks keep each property on schedule instead of on your to-do list.

Your contractor network, saved

Photograph a business card and it becomes a saved contact — your trusted plumber, electrician, and roofer, tied to the properties they've worked on. It's your network, not a lead-gen list.

Landlord questions

What owners ask

Can I manage more than one property?

Yes. Eave is free for one property and keeps a complete record for that home — projects, maintenance, appliances, and contractors. Eave Multi-Property extends that across 2–5 properties for $7.99/property/mo, and Portfolio (6+ properties) is available by talking to our team.

How much is multi-property?

Eave Multi-Property is $7.99/property/mo or $69/property/yr, for 2–5 properties. Your first property is free, and Eave Intelligence is $2.99 a month. Portfolio (6+ properties) is available by talking to our team.
