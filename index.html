Capture

Notice something?
Just say it.

Most home management apps make you navigate menus and fill forms. Eave starts with you. Speak a problem out loud, snap a photo, or type what you're thinking. Eave's AI parses it, structures it into a project — and shows it to you for review before saving anything.

From thought to filed project: under 5 seconds.

🎤 Speak it

"The master bathroom toilet keeps running — probably a flapper, maybe $150"

→ Project created. Room: Master Bath. Category: Plumbing. Est: $150–200.

📷 Snap it

Point at the problem. Eave's vision AI diagnoses the issue and generates the project record.

→ Photo analyzed. Cracked caulk detected. Category: Bathroom. Priority: Medium.

⌨️ Type it

Free-form notes in plain English. No forms, no menus.

→ Parsed: deck boards loose near steps. Priority: High (safety).

Memory

Your home
has a history.
Eave keeps it.

Every room. Every appliance. Every contractor you've ever used. Every repair you've made — what it cost, who did it, when it was done. Eave builds a complete, searchable record of your home over time, enriched by photos, receipts, and smart home connections.

Connect your Gmail and Eave quietly finds past Home Depot orders, appliance purchases, and service receipts — and pre-populates your home history for your review.

Room Inventory
Snap a room, Eave documents the floors, walls, finishes, and fixtures with AI confidence scores.
Appliance Registry
Track every major system with model, purchase date, warranty expiry, and service intervals.
Contractor Directory
Photograph a business card and Eave saves the contact. Your personal, trusted network — not cold lead-gen.
Connected Sources
Link Gmail, Home Depot, Lowe's, Amazon, and smart home platforms to auto-populate your home history.
