Intelligence

Know your score.
Act on your queue.
Stay a season ahead.

Your Upkeep Score is always on — free, live, and calculated across four dimensions: maintenance completion, project backlog health, preventive vs. reactive ratio, and how consistently you're using the app. It updates whenever you log work, complete a task, or clear a project. No manual entry needed.

Upgrade to Eave Intelligence and the score becomes the foundation for AI-driven action: a ranked project queue that tells you what to do next and why, a risk assessment for every deferred task, a personalized seasonal prep plan, and a 12-month care plan built from your home's actual data.
