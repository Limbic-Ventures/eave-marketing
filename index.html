How It Works

Set up in minutes.
Eave takes it
from there.

Give Eave your address. It pulls your home's age, size, and structure automatically, then tailors the plan to your climate and local maintenance windows.
02
Capture your first project
Speak, snap, or type something you've been meaning to deal with. See Eave structure it in real time.
03
Connect your accounts
Link your email, home improvement store accounts, or smart home platforms. Eave surfaces what it finds for your review — nothing saves without your say.
