Memory

Your home
has a history.
Eave keeps it.

Spaces

Spaces is your full property map — Structures (your main house, garage, or any outbuilding) and Grounds (yard, garden, pool area, orchard). Every structure contains its rooms; every ground area gets its own record. Your entire property, organized in one place.

Rooms

Inside each structure, document every room — finishes, materials, dimensions, fixtures, and linked appliances. Snap a photo and Eave fills in what it can. A searchable record of every surface and system in your home, room by room.

Appliance Registry

Every major system tracked with model, install date, warranty expiry, and service history. Eave flags overdue service and upcoming warranty expirations before they catch you off guard.

Tools & Materials

Track every finish, material, and spec used in your home — paint colors with brand and code, tile dimensions, grout color, lumber sizing, hardware finishes. Never wonder what paint a room is or what tile was used in the bathroom.

Contractor Directory

Photograph a business card and Eave saves the contact — tied to the project they worked on, the date, and what you paid. Your personal trusted network, built over time.

Connected Sources

Link your email, home improvement accounts, and smart home platforms. Eave surfaces appliance purchases, service receipts, and order history for your review — pre-populating your home record without any manual entry.

Intelligence

Know your score.
Act on your queue.
Stay a season ahead.

Upkeep Score FREE

A live 0–100 score calculated across four dimensions: maintenance completion, project backlog health, preventive vs. reactive ratio, and engagement consistency. Five tiers from Neglected to Excellent — always on, no manual entry needed.

See your 7-day delta and a projection of what completing any single task will do to your score.

Risk Assessment INTELLIGENCE

Every deferred task gets a risk profile: cost escalation over 30, 60, and 90 days; cascade damage potential — what else breaks if this isn't fixed; safety flags; and whether a seasonal window is closing. Know the real cost of waiting.

AI Project Queue INTELLIGENCE

Every open project ranked by urgency, with a one-sentence "why now" reason. Seasonal timing, cascade risk, safety flags, and risk assessment all factor in — so the most important task is always at the top.

Seasonal Prep Digest INTELLIGENCE

14 days before each season, Eave generates a personalized prep list for your home — not a generic checklist. Each task includes a reason, estimated hours, DIY vs. hire recommendation, and urgency level. Built from your actual appliances, history, and location.

12-Month Upkeep Plan INTELLIGENCE

Your home's full year, mapped out by AI. Month-by-month care tasks, seasonal prep windows, and project milestones — all generated from your Eave data and refreshed when your situation changes.
