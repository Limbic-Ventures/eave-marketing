Eave Partners · Real estate agents

Give your clients a home that runs itself.
And a reason to remember who sold it to them.

Eave is the home-memory app your clients keep long after closing. Help them show up to listing day ready, and stay their go-to for years.

Where Eave fits in your work

Useful before the listing, and long after

  1. Before listing — turn your prep list into their to-do list

    Every recommendation you make gets tracked, so sellers show up to photo day ready instead of scrambling. The dozen easy wins you rattle off at the walkthrough stop evaporating before listing.

  2. After closing — stay useful, not just in touch

    Your buyers keep a tool you introduced them to, and you stay top-of-mind for the next move and the referral. It's a gift that keeps working long after the closing gift is forgotten.

  3. Eave Leads — meet sellers before they're on Zillow

    Homeowners on Eave getting ready to list can choose to send their home's packet to an agent. It's the earliest, highest-intent seller signal there is — and it's the homeowner who initiates it.

How it works for you

Flat and predictable, start to finish

Cover your clients' subscription through the sale; they take it over when they close. Eave Leads is a flat monthly subscription — never a cut of your commission.

Eave never pays referral fees or shares commissions. If you have a financial interest in Eave, disclose it to your clients in writing.

For agents, answered

Questions, answered.

What does it cost me?

Flat and predictable. Cover your clients' subscriptions and/or subscribe to Eave Leads. There's no per-deal fee and nothing tied to a closing.

Is this a referral-fee arrangement?

No. Eave never pays you for referrals. Eave Leads is homeowner-initiated and priced as a flat subscription.

Can my brokerage roll this out?

Let's talk.

Bring Eave to your clients. Tell us a little about your practice and we'll be in touch.
