Give your clients a home that runs itself.
And a reason to remember who sold it to them.

Eave is the home-memory app your clients keep long after closing. Help them show up to listing day ready, and stay their go-to for years.

The relationship that usually fades

The closing gift ends up in a drawer.
The house doesn’t.

“I sold them a great house three years ago. I have no idea if they’d think to call me next time.”

Most of a client relationship happens in a few intense weeks, and then it goes quiet. The magnet on the fridge fades; the closing gift gets re-gifted. What stays useful is the house itself — and the one tool that keeps helping with it is the one they’ll remember you for. Eave is that tool: the home-memory app you hand your clients, working for them the whole time you’re not in front of them.

Before the listing

Turn your prep list into their to-do list

The easy wins stop evaporating

You walk a house a year out and rattle off a dozen easy wins — regrout the guest bath, service the furnace, repaint the garage door. By listing day, half of them are forgotten. When your seller captures each one in Eave the moment you say it, they show up to photo day ready instead of scrambling.

Eave sorts the list by value and effort for the time before listing, so the work gets done on their schedule — not the week the sign goes up. Fewer surprises at inspection, fewer price chips at the table.

Long after closing

Stay useful, not just in touch

The gift that keeps working

Your buyers move in and keep a tool you introduced them to — the searchable record of every system, appliance, warranty, and project in their new home. Every time it reminds them to change a filter or flags an aging water heater, it’s doing a small favor with your name on it.

When they’re ready for the next move, you’re not a name they have to dig for. You’re the person who gave them the thing they still use.

Eave Leads

Meet sellers before they’re on Zillow

Homeowners getting ready to list can choose to send their home’s packet to an agent — the projects, the records, the whole documented history. It’s the earliest, highest-intent seller signal there is, and the homeowner is always the one who starts it. Eave is the conduit, never the broker: you never buy a name, and nothing is tied to a closing.

Why it works

Give your clients a home
that runs itself.

A cared-for home is worth more, sells easier, and reflects well on the person who sold it. Eave is how you leave your clients with one — and how you stay the name attached to it.

How it works for you

Flat and predictable, start to finish

Cover your clients’ subscription through the sale; they take it over when they close. Eave Leads is a flat monthly subscription — never a cut of your commission. No per-deal fee, nothing contingent on a closing, no rankings or “preferred” badges to buy.

Eave never pays referral fees or shares commissions. If you have a financial interest in Eave, disclose it to your clients in writing.

For agents, answered

Questions, answered.

What does it cost me?

Flat and predictable. Cover your clients’ subscriptions and/or subscribe to Eave Leads. There’s no per-deal fee and nothing tied to a closing.

Is this a referral-fee arrangement?

No. Eave never pays you for referrals. Eave Leads is homeowner-initiated and priced as a flat subscription.

Does my client have to keep paying after closing?

Their choice. If you cover Intelligence through the sale, they simply take the subscription over at closing if they want to keep it — and the free tier keeps their home’s record either way. Nothing is ever held hostage.

What does the client actually get?

The full Eave Residential app: a searchable record of their home’s systems and appliances, an AI upkeep plan, reminders, and their Upkeep Score. It’s the same product a homeowner would sign up for on their own — you’re just the reason they have it.
