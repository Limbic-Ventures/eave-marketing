FAQ

Pricing questions

Is Eave really free?

Yes. Eave is free for one property, forever — no credit card and no trial expiry. Eave Intelligence and Eave Pro are optional add-ons you choose only if you want more.

What's the difference between Free and Eave Intelligence?

Free gives you the records and tools: projects, systems and appliances, spaces, budget tracking, contractors, your Upkeep Score, and connected sources. Intelligence adds the AI project queue, a 12-month upkeep plan, the seasonal prep digest, and materials estimates — the machinery that turns your records into a ranked plan.

How much does Eave Intelligence cost?

Eave Intelligence is $2.99/month, $24/year (about $2/month), or $49 once for lifetime access.

When will Multi-Property and Eave Pro be available?

Both are coming soon. Multi-property will be a separate add-on to Eave Intelligence for up to five properties — every plan covers one today. Eave Pro is a waitlist-only concierge offering, starting with a Home Audit and Seasonal Manager, arranged directly by our team. Pricing for both will be shared when they are available. Join the waitlist to hear first.

Can I cancel Intelligence anytime?

Yes. You can cancel anytime from Settings → Membership, and your home's records stay in the free tier — canceling never deletes your data. See Billing for details.
