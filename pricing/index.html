FAQ

Pricing questions

Is Eave really free?

Yes. Eave is free for one home, forever — no credit card and no trial expiry. Your records export at every tier, including Free.

What does Guardian add over Intelligence?

Guardian, launching this fall, adds the evidence vault with one-tap insurance and warranty claim packets, a 10-year capex forecast, the carrier-discount checklist, an annual virtual audit review, telemetry fusion for leak, electrical, solar, and thermostat sensors (rolling out), and priority support.

How much does Eave cost?

Eave Intelligence is $2.99/month or $24/year. Eave Guardian (launching this fall) is $24.99/month or $199/year. Multi-Property is $7.99/property/month or $69/property/year, and Portfolio (6+ properties) is available by talking to our team. Annual billing is the default across every tier.

I bought the $49 lifetime — what happens?

Nothing changes. Early Members keep Intelligence forever. New pricing applies to new signups only.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel anytime from Settings → Membership, and your home's records stay in the free tier — canceling never deletes your data. See Billing for details.
