FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is Eave really free?

Yes — free for one home, forever, with no credit card. Eave Intelligence is an optional upgrade at $2.99/mo, and Eave Guardian — active protection, coming this fall — will be an optional add-on on top. See full pricing →

Do I need to download an app?

No. Eave runs in any browser at app.eavehome.app — phone, tablet, or desktop. Native iOS and Android apps are coming soon. Getting started →

How does Eave capture what my home needs?

Speak it, snap a photo, or type a few words. Eave turns it into a scoped project — the room, the category, the priority, and an estimated cost. More on Capture →

Is my home data private?

Your data is yours. Nothing saves until you review it, it's stored with row-level security, and you can export or delete everything at any time. Data & privacy →

What does Eave Intelligence add?

A 12-month upkeep plan built for your home, a ranked “why-now” queue, and seasonal prep — the planning layer on top of your free records. More on Intelligence →

Can I manage more than one home?

Yes. Eave Multi-Property is $7.99/property/mo (or $69/property/yr) for 2–5 properties, and Portfolio (6+ properties) is available by talking to our team. Spaces & properties →

Can I pass my home’s record to the buyer when I sell?

Yes — your home’s memory can transfer with the sale, so the new owner starts with the full history instead of a blank slate. Getting ready to sell →
