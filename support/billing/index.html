Billing & plans

FAQs

Common questions

What plans does Eave offer?

Eave Free is $0 forever for one property — projects, tasks, systems & appliances, spaces, budget tracking, contractors, and your Upkeep Score, in any browser.

Eave Intelligence adds the AI project queue, 12-month upkeep plan, seasonal prep digest, and materials estimates from $2.99/month ($24/year, or $49 lifetime).

Eave Pro adds professional services, starting with licensed Home Audits. Multi-property is coming soon as an Intelligence add-on (+$39/yr per additional property). See Pricing for full details.

How do I manage my subscription?

Settings → Membership shows your plan and billing. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime.

Do you offer refunds?

If something's not right, email us within 14 days of a charge and we'll make it right.

In Depth

Managing your membership

Where to find your plan and billing details

Go to Settings → Membership to view your current plan, next billing date, and payment method. You can upgrade or cancel from there at any time.

Monthly, annual, and lifetime options

Eave Intelligence is available monthly ($2.99), annually ($24), or as a one-time $49 lifetime purchase. You can switch plans from Settings → Membership.

Canceling

Cancel anytime from Settings → Membership. Your home's records stay in the free tier — canceling never deletes your data.
