Your 12-month care plan

An AI-generated 12-month upkeep schedule built from your real home profile (an Intelligence feature).

Your care plan is a rolling 12-month schedule of upkeep for your home, built from what Eave already knows about it. It's here so you don't have to keep the whole year in your head — Eave remembers, and surfaces the right task at the right time.

You'll find it under Upkeep Plan in the sidebar.

What it is

The Upkeep Plan lays out seasonal maintenance across the next twelve months, spread so nothing bunches up and nothing gets forgotten. Instead of a generic checklist, it reflects your actual home — its systems, appliances, spaces, and location — so the tasks it suggests are ones that genuinely apply to you.

How it's generated

Eave builds the plan from your home's real profile, not a one-size-fits-all template:

  • Your systems and appliances — items in Systems & Appliances and Tools & Materials, along with their maintenance intervals and part specs, feed directly into the schedule.
  • Your spaces and inventory — the layout and contents of your home shape which tasks are relevant.
  • Your location — Eave pulls public data for your address (climate, freeze dates, rainfall, air quality, and local risk factors) to time seasonal work sensibly. For example, air-quality data helps schedule HVAC filter changes.

Tasks land on your calendar within their proper season, so outdoor work shows up before the weather turns and indoor tasks fall where they make sense.

How it refreshes

The plan isn't static. As your home changes — you add an appliance, import an inspection report, connect a new source, or complete work — Eave folds that in. Reminders that support the plan are regenerated nightly from your home's current state. You can also revisit the Upkeep Plan page any time to see the schedule as it stands today. Nothing is added to your plan without you; suggested upkeep is always something you choose to accept.

This is an Intelligence feature

The Upkeep Plan is part of Eave Intelligence, the paid tier that covers Eave's AI planning features. Intelligence is:

  • $2.99/month
  • $24/year (about $2/month)
  • $49 once (lifetime — never expires)

To subscribe, open the profile menu, go to Membership, and pick a plan. Checkout is handled by Stripe.

Your free tools — Projects, Tasks, Appliances, Spaces, Budget, Contractors, and your Upkeep Score — stay free and keep working whether or not you have Intelligence.

Common questions

Quick answers

Is my Upkeep Score the same as the Upkeep Plan?

No. The Upkeep Score is a free 0–100 measure of how well your home is being kept up. The Upkeep Plan is the Intelligence feature that schedules the year's work.

Do I have to do every task Eave suggests?

No. Suggestions are yours to accept or skip — nothing is forced onto your plan.

Will the plan update if I add a new appliance?

Yes. As your home profile changes, the plan and its reminders adjust to match.

What happens to my plan if I cancel Intelligence?

Access to the Upkeep Plan pauses, but your free tools and all your saved data remain intact.
