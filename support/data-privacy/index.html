Support · Data & Privacy

Your home's data stays yours

What Eave reads (and refuses to read), how your records are protected, and how to export or delete everything.

FAQs

Common questions

Is my data private?

Yes. Your data is stored with Row-Level Security so only you can access it. Photos go to a private storage bucket. Nothing is shared or sold.

Can I export my home data?

Full export (for resale packages, insurance, or refinance) is on the roadmap. Your data always belongs to you.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account, or email us and we'll handle it. Deletion removes all your records permanently.

What does Eave read from my connected email?

Eave reads emails that match home-related patterns (receipts, appliance purchases, contractor invoices). It does not read personal correspondence, financial accounts, or anything outside the home category.

In Depth

How your data is handled

You review everything before it saves

Connected-source imports (email, store accounts) always land in a review queue first. Nothing saves to your home's record without your confirmation — you approve, edit, or dismiss each item.

Privacy, data ownership, and security

Your data is stored with row-level security — only your account can access it. We don't sell or share your data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Exporting or deleting your data

Export is on the roadmap. To delete your account and all associated data, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account, or contact us directly.
