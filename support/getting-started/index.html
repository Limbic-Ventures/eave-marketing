Getting started with Eave

What Eave is, what it costs, and how to get your home set up — from your property profile to your first appliance record.

FAQs

Common questions

What is Eave?

Eave is your home's memory. It tracks your property, appliances, rooms, projects, and maintenance in one place, and uses AI to turn a quick note or photo into organized, actionable home records.

Do I need to download an app?

No. Eave runs in your browser at app.eavehome.app — phone, tablet, or desktop. Native iOS and Android apps are coming soon.

How much does Eave cost?

Eave is free for one property, forever. Eave Intelligence — the AI upkeep plan, seasonal prep, and ranked priorities — starts at $2.99/month ($24/year, or $49 lifetime). Eave Pro adds professional services like licensed Home Audits. See Pricing for current details, or the Billing page for plan management.

Is Eave available outside the US?

The core app works anywhere. Some data enrichment (property records, risk data) is US-only for now.

In Depth

Setting up your home

Creating your property profile

After signing in, tap "Add Property" and enter your address. Eave pre-fills public data like square footage and year built — review and correct anything that looks off before saving.

Adding Spaces and Rooms

In Memory, tap "+ Add Space" to create a structure (Main House, Garage, ADU). Inside each Space, tap "+ Add Room" and choose a room type to start documenting.

A Space is a distinct structure on your property; Rooms live inside Spaces. The Memory Builder documents every room within each space.

Documenting appliances and systems

Open any room and scroll to Appliances. Tap "+ Add" and enter the make, model, and install year — or snap the data plate and let Eave identify the unit and auto-research its specs, down to consumable sizes like furnace filters. Eave uses this for maintenance reminders and warranty tracking.

Understanding your Home Score

Your Home Score reflects documentation completeness and maintenance posture. It improves as you add rooms, log maintenance, and resolve open projects.
