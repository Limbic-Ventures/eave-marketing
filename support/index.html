Knowledge Base

Help articles by topic

Setting Up Your Home
Creating your property profile
After signing in, tap "Add Property" and enter your address. Eave pre-fills public data like square footage and year built — review and correct anything that looks off before saving.
Adding Spaces and Rooms
In Memory, tap "+ Add Space" to create a structure (Main House, Garage, ADU). Inside each Space, tap "+ Add Room" and choose a room type to start documenting.
Documenting appliances and systems
Open any room and scroll to Appliances. Tap "+ Add" and enter the make, model, and install year. Eave uses this for maintenance reminders and warranty tracking.
Understanding your Home Score
Your Home Score reflects documentation completeness and maintenance posture. It improves as you add rooms, log maintenance, and resolve open projects.
AI Features
How AI Capture turns notes into projects
Type or speak a description in the Capture tab. Eave's AI parses it into structured fields — title, room, category, priority, estimated cost, and permit flag — then queues it for your review.
Scanning rooms with photo analysis
Open a room in Memory and tap "Scan Room." Your photo is uploaded and analyzed. AI fills in flooring, wall color, ceiling height, and flags visible issues. Everything is editable.
Reviewing and correcting AI suggestions
AI suggestions appear in a review queue before saving. Tap any field to edit. Corrections help Eave improve over time.
Generating shopping lists from projects
On any project, tap "Generate List." Eave produces a materials and tools list based on the project description. You can edit, copy, or export it.
Connected Sources
Connecting Gmail for receipt and warranty detection
Go to Connected Sources and tap "Connect Gmail." Authorize read-only access. Eave scans for home-related emails — receipts, warranties, contractor confirmations.
Importing past purchases
After connecting a source, detected items appear in your review queue. Tap "Add to Eave" on any item to save it. Nothing imports without your confirmation.
What Eave does and doesn't read
Eave reads emails that match home-related patterns (receipts, appliance purchases, contractor invoices). It does not read personal correspondence, financial accounts, or anything outside the home category.
Maintenance & Projects
Seasonal maintenance tracker
The Maintenance tab shows a checklist of seasonal tasks based on your property type and location. Check off tasks as you complete them — Eave logs the date automatically.
Project statuses, priorities, and filters
Projects can be New, In Progress, or Done. Priority levels are Low, Medium, High, and Urgent. Use filters in the Intelligence tab to sort and surface what needs attention.
Resolution paths: DIY, Learn, or Hire
Each project has a resolution path. DIY means you're handling it. Learn shows relevant guides. Hire surfaces contractors from your directory or the Eave network.
The contractor directory
Add contractors you've used under Contacts. Include specialty, notes, and rating. When a project needs a hire, Eave surfaces relevant contacts from your directory first.
Account & Billing
Managing your membership
Go to Settings → Membership to view your current plan, next billing date, and payment method. You can upgrade or cancel from there at any time.
Privacy, data ownership, and security
Your data is stored with row-level security — only your account can access it. We don't sell or share your data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Exporting or deleting your data
Export is on the roadmap. To delete your account and all associated data, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account, or contact us directly.

Tutorials

Step-by-step guides

01 Your first 10 minutes in Eave
Video walkthrough coming soon
  1. Sign up at app.eavehome.app using your email or Google account.
  2. When prompted, tap "Add Property" and enter your address. Review the pre-filled details and save.
  3. Open the Memory tab and tap "+ Add Space." Choose "Main House" and save.
  4. Inside Main House, tap "+ Add Room," pick a room type (e.g. Kitchen), and save.
  5. Open the Capture tab, type or speak a project description (e.g. "fix the leaky kitchen faucet"), and tap Capture.
  6. Review the AI-parsed fields — title, room, priority, estimated cost. Edit anything that's off, then tap "Add to Queue."
  7. Head to the Intelligence tab to see your new project in the queue.
02 Turning a photo into a room record
Video walkthrough coming soon
  1. Open the Memory tab and navigate to the room you want to document.
  2. Tap "Scan Room" at the top of the room screen.
  3. Take a photo or upload one from your camera roll. Try to capture as much of the room as possible in a single shot.
  4. Wait a moment while Eave's AI analyzes the photo — this usually takes 10–20 seconds.
  5. Review the suggested fields: floor material, wall color, ceiling height, and any flagged issues.
  6. Edit anything that looks incorrect. Corrections help improve future scans.
  7. Tap Save. The room record is now updated with the scanned details.
03 Capturing a project by voice or text
Video walkthrough coming soon
  1. Tap the Capture tab at the bottom of the screen.
  2. Type a description of the project in plain language — or tap the microphone icon and speak it. Don't worry about format; just describe what needs to happen.
  3. Tap "Capture." Eave's AI parses the description and fills in the structured fields.
  4. Review the parsed result: title, room assignment, category, priority, estimated cost, and permit flag.
  5. Edit any field that's off. You can also add notes or change the room assignment here.
  6. Tap "Add to Queue." The project appears in your Intelligence tab, ready for triage.
04 Connecting Gmail to auto-find receipts
Video walkthrough coming soon
  1. Go to Settings → Connected Sources.
  2. Tap "Connect Gmail" and sign in with your Google account.
  3. Grant read-only access when prompted. Eave only reads emails — it cannot send or delete anything.
  4. Eave scans for home-related emails: receipts, warranties, contractor confirmations, appliance purchases.
  5. Detected items appear in your review queue. Tap any item to see the full email context.
  6. Tap "Add to Eave" to save an item, or "Skip" to dismiss it. Nothing saves without your confirmation.
05 Setting up seasonal maintenance
Video walkthrough coming soon
  1. Open the Maintenance tab.
  2. Eave generates a seasonal checklist based on your property type, region, and current month.
  3. Review the list. Each task shows what it involves and why it matters for your home type.
  4. Tap any task to expand it and see steps or notes.
  5. When you complete a task, tap the checkbox. Eave logs the completion date automatically.
  6. Completed tasks roll over to the next season's history so you always have a maintenance record.
