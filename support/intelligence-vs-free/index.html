Eave Free vs Eave Intelligence

Exactly what's included in each plan today, and what Intelligence adds.

Eave is your home's memory, and that memory is yours to keep no matter which plan you're on. What Intelligence adds is the AI planning on top of your records: a 12-month schedule, smart prioritizing, cost estimates, and finding local pros. This page lays out exactly what's in each plan today, and what it costs.

What Free includes

Everything you need to keep your home's records in one place, with no time limit:

  • Projects — track and manage all your home work
  • Tasks — a recurring maintenance scheduler
  • Systems & Appliances — a registry with warranty tracking and reminders
  • Spaces & Areas — your home layout and room inventory
  • Budgets — cost tracking and actuals
  • Contractors — your contact book
  • Upkeep Score — a simple 0–100 read on how well your home is being kept up

You also get the everyday AI helpers on Free: Quick Capture (type, speak, or snap a photo of a project), photo analysis, document import (inspection reports, manuals, warranties, and insurance pages), Connected Sources including the Gmail receipt scan, "Enrich with AI" on appliances, and shopping-list generation. These run AI but aren't gated by plan — they're only subject to generous daily limits.

What Eave Intelligence unlocks

Intelligence turns your records into a plan:

  • Upkeep Plan — an AI-generated 12-month maintenance schedule
  • AI Project Queue — your projects prioritized, with plain "why now" reasoning
  • Materials Estimates — a DIY cost breakdown per project
  • AI Vendor Research — find local pros for a specific project
  • AI calendar tasks — intelligent seasonal recommendations added to your schedule
  • Multiple properties *(coming soon)* — a separate add-on to manage up to 5 homes; every plan covers 1 today

Pricing

Eave Intelligence, self-serve:

  • $2.99/month
  • $24/year (about $2/month)

The $49 lifetime option is no longer sold. If you bought it, nothing changes — Early Members keep Intelligence forever.

To subscribe, open your profile menu and go to Membership, then choose a plan. Checkout runs through Stripe.

Your records stay yours

We gate the intelligence, never your memory. If you cancel Intelligence or a subscription lapses, everything you've entered — projects, appliances, documents, contractors, history — stays exactly where it is. You simply return to the Free feature set. A lifetime purchase never expires. And at every tier, including Free, you can download a full JSON export of everything you own from My Account.

Common questions

Quick answers

Do I lose my data if I downgrade?

No. Your records are always available. Only the AI planning features turn off.

What's the difference between the Upkeep Score and the Upkeep Plan?

The Score is a free health read-out. The Plan is the Intelligence feature that builds your 12-month schedule.

How do I cancel or update my card?

Membership page → Manage Billing opens the Stripe portal, where you can cancel, update payment, or download invoices.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds aren't self-serve — reach out to support@eavehome.app and the team will help.

Can I manage more than one property?

Every plan covers one property today. Multi-property support (up to five) is coming soon as a separate add-on.
